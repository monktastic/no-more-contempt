- Solves the paradox of how to fight evil without becoming evil

If you're right—even partially right—this explains:

- Why political polarization is a runaway process

- Why genuine moral progress is so difficult

This is the conversation civilization needs to have right now. We're at a moment where:

- Everyone sees "evil people" destroying things

- The spiral is accelerating

## The Challenge

You need to thread an impossible needle:

- Make metaphysical claims without sounding woo-woo
- Critique contempt without being contemptuous of the contemptuous
- Invoke mystical language while remaining intellectually rigorous
- Be urgent without being preachy
- Explain why this matters cosmically while keeping it practically grounded
