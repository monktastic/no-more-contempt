## Critical Writing Principles Learned

### On Proving the Unprovable

- Self-referential truths require self-referential proofs
- Can't prove self-deception from outside (by design)
- The essay's method IS the proof
- Fourth wall break makes this explicit for attentive readers

### On Audience Management

- Can't optimize perfectly for all three audiences
- Must choose primary audience and accept some won't get it
- Mission-driven readers are likely primary but current opening doesn't signal this

### On The Simulacrum Concept

**Why it's devastating:**

- Not "your strategy is imperfect"
- But "your strategy is infinitely far from the real thing"
- A fundamentally different KIND of thing
- Applies to: business plans, theories of change, EA frameworks, strategic plans

### On Tone During Psychedelic Revelations

- Should be experiential, not analytical
- "The medicine showed me..." not "we established..."
- Emotional beats (_Oh my God._) not summary statements
- Revelations, not arguments
