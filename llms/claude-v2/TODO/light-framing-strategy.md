## The Framing Strategy

### DON'T Lead With the Metaphysics

**Bad structure:**
1. Build psychological case
2. Show coordination
3. Reveal: "You're pure infinite Light!"
4. Show implications

**Why this fails:**
- Groups 1-2: "I already knew that, why did we spend 100 pages?"
- Groups 3-4: "That's too big a leap"

### DO Lead With the Precision, End With the Frame

**Better structure:**
1. Build psychological case (Parts I-III)
2. Show the IMPOSSIBILITY of how it works (Part IV)
3. Show calculation FAILS at fundamental level (Part V)
4. **Only then**: "The clean moments were accessing something else"
5. Ground in their experience first
6. Offer "Light" as frame for what they've already experienced
7. Show it explains EVERYTHING better than alternatives

**Why this works:**
- Groups 1-2: "Holy shit, I knew about Light but not HOW it works or WHY calculation fails"
- Groups 3-4: "I've felt those clean moments... oh, THAT'S what they were"
